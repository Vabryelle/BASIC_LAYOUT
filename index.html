✴Jujutsu Kaisen✴

Jujutsu Kaisen is one of the recent animes that was released in October of 2020- March of 2021. It is rapidly raiseing in the ratings, and has easily became peoples favorite. A new movie related to the series was recently announced to be realesed December 24,2021. MAPPA studio, also known for creating Attack On Titan, is creating this series. The story is about a boy names Yuji Itadori who accidently swallows a high level curse's finger. This gave him the power to actually see curses throughout the world that is blind to the naked eye. Itadori must fight through the hardships and responsibilities in order to save the world from curses.
